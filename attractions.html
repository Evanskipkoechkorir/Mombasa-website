Explore the Best of Mombasa

From ancient forts to stunning beaches, Mombasa has something for every traveler. Here are some must-visit attractions:

🏰 Fort Jesus

Built by the Portuguese in the 16th century, Fort Jesus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It reflects Mombasa’s rich history of trade, culture, and colonial struggles.

🌆 Old Town

Wander through narrow streets filled with Swahili, Arab, and European architecture. Old Town is famous for its spice markets, antique shops, and traditional doors.

🏖️ Nyali & Diani Beaches

Relax on the pristine white sand beaches with turquoise waters. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, snorkeling, and water sports.

🦐 Mombasa Tusks

The iconic landmark along Moi Avenue, built in 1952 to commemorate Queen Elizabeth II’s visit. The tusks symbolize Mombasa’s rich history as a gateway city.
